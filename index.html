Rules

Be Better than Yesterday.

  • If you fail, you start from day one.
  • You can study more than the daily target if it won't affect your studying the next day.
  • Each day, aim to study 5% more than you did the previous day.
  • Record your daily progress to track your improvement.
  • Make adjustments to your study plan as needed to ensure sustainable progress.
  • Celebrate milestones and achievements along the way to stay motivated.

Elevate Your Study Habits, One Day at a Time. Embarking on a 30-day study challenge empowers you to enhance your learning habits gradually. Starting with just one hour of focused study, you gradually increase your study time each day. Along the way, you'll experience the benefits of consistent practice and disciplined effort.

  • Starting Small: Begin with one hour of dedicated study time daily.
  • Gradual Increase: Incrementally add more time to your study sessions.
  • Consistent Practice: Transform studying into a daily routine, akin to brushing your teeth.
  • Enhanced Focus: Develop better concentration and discipline through regular study sessions.
  • Building Strong Habits: Establish robust study habits that pave the way for long-term success.
